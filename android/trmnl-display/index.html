TRMNL Display Android App

Control and manage your TRMNL e-ink display from your Android device.

TRMNL Display is an Android app that allows you to control and manage your TRMNL e-ink display devices. Configure settings, update content, and monitor your displays all from your Android device.

Key Features:

  • Device Control: Manage and configure your TRMNL e-ink display devices directly from your Android device.
  • Content Management: Update and customize the content displayed on your TRMNL devices.
  • Settings Configuration: Adjust display settings, refresh rates, and other device parameters.
  • Real-time Updates: Push updates to your TRMNL displays instantly from your phone.
  • Modern Android Design: Built with the latest Android development practices and Material Design.
  • Open Source: Fully open source project available on GitHub for transparency and community contributions.

Why Choose TRMNL Display?

  • Seamless control of your TRMNL e-ink displays from anywhere.
  • Intuitive interface designed for ease of use.
  • Regular updates and improvements from the open source community.
  • Compatible with all TRMNL display devices.

Technical Highlights:

  • Built with modern Android development tools
  • Material Design user interface
  • Efficient e-ink display communication
  • Open source and community-driven
