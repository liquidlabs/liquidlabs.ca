H.K. Vision - Wallpapers Android App

Beautiful wallpapers for your Android device

This is a plugin for "Muzei Live Wallpaper" Android application that allows you to use wallpapers from H.K. Vision website (vision.hossainkhan.com).

Key Features:

  • Muzei Integration: Seamlessly integrates with the popular Muzei Live Wallpaper app to bring beautiful wallpapers to your device.
  • Curated Collection: Access a carefully curated collection of high-quality wallpapers from H.K. Vision.
  • Automatic Updates: Your wallpaper changes automatically based on your Muzei settings, keeping your home screen fresh.
  • Easy to Use: Simply install the plugin and configure it within the Muzei app.
  • High Quality Images: All wallpapers are optimized for mobile devices with high resolution.

How to Use:

  1. Install Muzei Live Wallpaper from the Play Store (if you haven't already).
  2. Install the H.K. Vision - Wallpapers plugin.
  3. Open Muzei and select "H.K. Vision" as your wallpaper source.
  4. Configure your preferences (update frequency, etc.).
  5. Enjoy beautiful wallpapers on your device!

About H.K. Vision:

H.K. Vision is a platform dedicated to showcasing beautiful photography and artistic wallpapers. Visit vision.hossainkhan.com to explore the full collection.
