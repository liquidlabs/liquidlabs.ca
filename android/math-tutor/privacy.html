Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Liquid Labs Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use Math Pup Tutor. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Children's Privacy

Math Pup Tutor is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically: The app may collect non-personal information such as device type, operating system version, and app usage statistics to improve performance and user experience.

Information You Provide: Users may optionally create a profile with a username (no email required) to track learning progress. This information is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to our servers unless explicitly backed up by the user.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the Math Pup Tutor app functionality
  • Track learning progress and adapt the curriculum to the user's skill level
  • Analyze app performance and fix technical issues
  • Ensure a safe and appropriate learning environment

Data Storage and Security

All user progress data is stored locally on the device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

Math Pup Tutor may use third-party services for analytics or crash reporting. These services are COPPA-compliant and do not collect personally identifiable information from children.

Advertisements

Math Pup Tutor is ad-free and does not display third-party advertisements to protect children from inappropriate content and tracking.

Parental Controls

Parents can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application or using the "Reset Progress" feature in the app settings.

Your Rights

Parents have the right to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
