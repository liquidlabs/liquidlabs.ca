TRMNL Buddy Android App

Your companion app to monitor your TRMNLs on the go.

TRMNL Buddy is an Android app that lets you monitor your TRMNL e-ink display devices remotely. View device status, battery levels, WiFi signal strength, and current screen displays - all from your Android device.

Key Features:

  • Device Management: View and monitor all your TRMNL e-ink display devices in one place.
  • Real-time Status Monitoring: Check battery level, WiFi signal strength, and device health at a glance.
  • Device Preview: View the current screen display for devices with configured device tokens.
  • Battery History Tracking: Automatic weekly collection of battery data for health monitoring with historical charts.
  • Privacy-First Design: Obfuscated display of sensitive information (Device IDs and MAC addresses).
  • Material You Support: Full Material Design 3 with dynamic color theming from Android 12+.
  • Dark Mode: Automatic theme switching based on system preferences.
  • Secure Storage: Encrypted DataStore for API tokens and device tokens.

Why Choose TRMNL Buddy?

  • Monitor your TRMNL devices remotely without accessing a web browser.
  • Track battery health over time to anticipate when devices need charging.
  • Modern Android app built with the latest technologies and Material Design 3.
  • Open source project available on GitHub.

Technical Highlights:

  • Built with Jetpack Compose and Material Design 3
  • Circuit architecture for type-safe navigation
  • Metro dependency injection with KSP
  • Secure encrypted data storage
